Frequently Asked

Do I need the sensor hardware to use the app?
Live detection requires a Field Detect sensor unit. To explore the app without hardware, open Settings and enable Training Mode — a full simulated detection environment.

How do I connect to my sensor?
Join the sensor's Wi-Fi network on your phone, then open the app. Your phone keeps its cellular internet connection while linked to the sensor.

Why does the app ask for background location?
Background location keeps distance and bearing to detected aircraft current so lock-screen alerts stay accurate while your phone is pocketed. It is optional and controlled by the Background Scanning setting.

Is my data uploaded anywhere?
No. Detection data stays between your phone and your sensor. See our privacy policy.
