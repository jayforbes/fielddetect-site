Field Detect — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

Field Detect ("the app") is developed by Jay Forbes. This policy describes what information the app handles and how.

Information We Collect

Field Detect does not require an account and does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. There are no analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks in the app.

Location Data

The app uses your device's location solely to calculate distance and bearing between you and detected aircraft or their operators, and to position you on the map. With your permission, location may be used in the background so alerts remain accurate while the app is not on screen. Location data is processed on your device and is not transmitted to us or to any third party.

Detection Data

Drone detection information (Remote ID broadcast contents such as serial numbers, positions, and altitudes) is received by your Field Detect sensor hardware and displayed in the app over a direct local network connection. This data, along with any notes or labels you create, is stored locally on your device and on your sensor unit. It is not uploaded to any server operated by us.

Data Sharing

The app shares data only when you explicitly choose to (for example, exporting a CSV file or sharing a location via Messages). We have no access to that data.

Children

Field Detect is a professional tool and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; the current version will always be available at this URL.
